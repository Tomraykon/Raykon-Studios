RAYKON STUDIOS™

WHAT WILL RAYKON STUDIOS™ BE?

Raykon Studios™ is going to be an independent creative studio focused on game development, web development, and graphic design. It will work on creating games, websites, and designs with an emphasis on creativity and quality. The mission includes establishing Raykon Studios™ as a recognized trademark and completing a full game project using Unreal Engine. Although it's just starting, the goal is to grow as a studio and build our own community.
